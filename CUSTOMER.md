# Frost Cannabis — Customer Profile

## Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

**Who they are:**
- Adults 25-40, working professionals, regular cannabis consumers
- They know what they like and they're tired of being burned by inconsistent products
- They shop at dispensaries in Washington state
- They're not chasing trends — they want something reliable they can come back to every time
- Mainstream consumers, not connoisseurs or snobs — real people who smoke

**What they buy:**
- Full product line: flower, pre-rolls, concentrates, vapes, edibles
- They rotate between products depending on the occasion
- They want affordable quality — good weed at a fair price, not overpriced hype

**How they shop:**
- Walk into a dispensary and look for brands they recognize and trust
- Ask the budtender "what's actually good?" and want an honest answer
- Won't pay extra just for fancy packaging — they want the product to speak for itself
- When they find a brand that's consistent, they stick with it

## Pain Points

**What frustrates them about other brands:**
- **Overpriced hype brands** — paying $60 for an eighth because of marketing and packaging, not quality
- **Inconsistency** — bought the same strain last month and it was completely different
- **Can't tell what's real** — every brand claims to be "premium" and "craft" but most of it is average
- **Too many options, not enough trust** — the dispensary shelf is overwhelming with brands that all look the same

## What They Value

- **Consistent quality** — same strain, same experience, every time. This is the #1 thing.
- **Fair pricing** — quality doesn't have to mean expensive. Affordable and good.
- **Honesty** — no fake hype, no inflated THC claims, no pretending to be something you're not
- **Clean product** — properly grown, properly tested, no shortcuts

## Anti-Personas (Who Frost Is NOT For)

- **Snobby upscale customers** — people who only buy the most expensive option to flex. Frost isn't trying to be a luxury brand.
- **Trend chasers** — people who buy whatever's hyped on social media this week. Frost is for people who want reliability, not novelty.
- **Cannabis tourists** — one-time buyers looking for the wildest experience. Frost is for regulars.

## How They Talk

- Casual, direct language — no corporate speak, no cannabis industry jargon like "cultivar" or "terpene profile" unless it's useful
- They say things like: "This is solid," "I always grab Frost," "You know what you're getting"
- They don't say: "Exquisite notes of pine," "Artisanal small-batch craft," "Elevated experience"
- They appreciate brands that are straightforward and don't try too hard
