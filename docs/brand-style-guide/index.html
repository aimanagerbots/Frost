Brand Applications

How the Frost brand comes to life across digital products, patterns, and interfaces.

Dashboard Interface

Dark-first design with glassmorphism, sky blue accents, and card-based layouts. The sidebar uses backdrop-blur for depth.

Brand Pattern

Snowflake-inspired geometric pattern using the brand gradient. Use as hero backgrounds, section dividers, or promotional materials.

05

Dark Theme Guidelines

Frost is a dark-first brand. The dark theme is the primary experience, not an afterthought.

Backgrounds

Use #0F0F1A for page base, #1A1A2E for cards and elevated surfaces, #16162A for intermediate layers.

Borders

Subtle borders at #2A2A4A. Never use stark white or gray borders. Borders should separate, not dominate.

Text Hierarchy

#E8E8F0 for primary text, #8888AA for muted/secondary text. Never use pure white (#FFF) for body text.

Accent Usage

Frost Blue (#56C1E6) for interactive elements, active states, and primary actions. Use sparingly for maximum impact.

Glassmorphism

Sidebar and overlays use backdrop-blur-xl with slight transparency. Creates depth without heavy shadows.

Cards

rounded-xl (16px), 1px border, bg-card surface. Consistent across all modules. No drop shadows.

06

Voice & Tone

Frost speaks like a brilliant, calm assistant who respects your time and intelligence.

Do

Be concise and direct. Lead with the answer. Use clear, confident language. Feel helpful without being servile. Sound like a trusted advisor.

Don't

Don't use filler words or corporate jargon. Don't be overly enthusiastic or robotic. Never say "I'm just an AI" or apologize unnecessarily.

Personality

Cool, intelligent, helpful. Think of a brilliant friend who happens to know everything. Warm but not bubbly. Precise but not cold.

Avoid

Excessive emojis. Exclamation marks in every sentence. Generic AI phrases like "Great question!" or "I'd be happy to help!" Just help.
